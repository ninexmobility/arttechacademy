Tiffany Worland

Lead Administrator / Arts & Language / General Education.

mother - wife - photographer - student

Growing up in TX, Tiffany found her place in the world behind the lens of a camera. Holding dual degrees in Fabric Arts & Photography from the University of North Texas, Tiffany has done shoots in New York City, San Franscico, Dallas, & the Bahamas.

After givng birth to her first son in 2010, Tiffany immediately immersed herself in early childhood education and best practices for raising children and quickly learned about Attachment Parenting. The AP lifestyle birthed many new found ideas and led Tiffany and WIll to a system called Whole Life Radical Un-Schooling.

arTTech Acadamy is the "School" for purposes of reporting to officail agencies as needed.

Will Worland

Administrator / S.T.E.M. / General Education.

father - husband - engineer - student

Originally from Missouri, now a proud resident of California, Will has lived in MO, KS, WA, TX & now CA. He's visited every state west of the Mississippi River as well as NY, NJ, GA, TN, Canada & Mexico. A lover of travel and exploration, Will's hobbies include; primitive survival, camping, hiking, and learning about animals.

On the other end of the spectrum, Will has been working in the Information Technology space, specializing in Mobile Communications for over 20 years. Companies like Samsung, Apple, Southwest Airlines and Uber, have all relied on him at one time or another to support, design, engineer or deploy some of the most innovative technologies on the planet.

Will holds multiple industry certifications. For more details, please visit: willworland.com.
